Re-work: Product to be corrected to meet requirements Scrap: Product rejected as unsuitable Re-grade: Product is suitable for an alternative application where the product meets criteria Return: Items to be returned. Typically applies to purchased product Accept As Is: Product does not meet requirements, yet determined to meet "fitness for purpose". Requires written Product Manager Approval. E-mail is acceptable written approval Sort: Product to be sorted per action required Meets Spec: Product found to meet spec
